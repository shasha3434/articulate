# Articulate! Digital Edition 🎲

A digital version of the Articulate board game, built to replace the physical board, cards, and timer while keeping the human-to-human gameplay intact.

## How to Play

Visit: **https://shasha3434.github.io/articulate/**

1. Enter player names and choose number of teams
2. Players are randomly allocated to teams
3. Each turn: describer sees words on their device, teammates guess
4. Movement = number of correct guesses
5. Land on ♠ Spades = free-for-all round
6. First team to reach FINISH and win their Finish Spade wins!

## Features

- 🎯 **1,250 words** across 6 categories (Person, World, Object, Action, Nature, Random)
- ⏱️ **32-second timer** with audio buzzer
- ♠ **Spades mechanic** — free-for-all rounds with tie breakers
- 🏁 **Finish line spade** — complex win condition with battle-off support
- 📱 **Mobile-friendly** — designed for phone pass-around play
- ⚡ **No server needed** — runs entirely in the browser

## House Rules

- No dice — movement based on correct guesses
- One skip per turn (reclaimable if you go back and get it right)
- Strategic end-turn-early option with projected position display
- Spade winner gets next turn on their current square
